hey! i'm Tyler.

I started my career as a front-end developer, building web experiences at an agency. Working alongside designers and clients, I loved bringing ideas to life on the web. But over time, I realized I wanted to be part of the creative process from the start.

That’s when I made the leap into design, joining Sleeper about four years ago. I traded coding for pixels, personas, and product design, learning that great design is a mix of aesthetics, usability, and moments of delight. I enjoy the challenge to bring a sense of nostalgia to something unfamiliar.
